homeruns.2000(LearnEDA)R Documentation

Team home run numbers for different seasons.

Description

Total number of home runs hit all teams in the major league baseball league for the years 1900,1910,..., 2000.

Usage

homeruns.2000

Format

A data frame with 210 observations on the following 2 variables.

YEARS
season
HOMERUNS
count of team home runs

Source

Sean Lahman's database at www.baseball1.com
