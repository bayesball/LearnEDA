immigrants(LearnEDA)R Documentation

Immigrant counts to US.

Description

Immigrant counts to US from various countries.

Usage

immigrants

Format

A data frame with 41 observations on the following 3 variables.

Country
name of country
Region
region of the world
X1998.Count
the count of immigrants in 1998

Source

2001 New York Times Almanac
