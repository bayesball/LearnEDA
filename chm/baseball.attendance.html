baseball.attendance(LearnEDA)R Documentation

Attendance at baseball teams.

Description

Average attendance for home and away games for all major league baseball games in the 2001 season.

Usage

baseball.attendance

Format

A data frame with 30 observations on the following 4 variables.

Team
name of team
League
league where the team belongs
Home.Attendance
average attendance for home games
Away.Attendance
average attendance for away games

Source

www.espn.com web site
