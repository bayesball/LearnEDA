salaries(LearnEDA)R Documentation

Salaries of different professions in different cities.

Description

1979 salaries of seven different professions in six cities.

Usage

salaries

Format

A data frame with 42 observations on the following 3 variables.

Salary
salary in hundreds of Swiss francs
City
name of city
Profession
name of profession

Source

unknown
