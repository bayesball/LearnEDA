boston.marathon(LearnEDA)R Documentation

Boston Marathon completion times of women of different ages.

Description

Completion times for a sample of women at the 2001 Boston Marathon.

Usage

boston.marathon

Format

A data frame with 108 observations on the following 2 variables.

time
completion time in minutes
age
age of the participant

Source

www.bostonmarathon.org website
