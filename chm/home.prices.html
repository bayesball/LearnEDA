home.prices(LearnEDA)R Documentation

Home sales prices in the U.S.

Description

Average sales prices of houses for 21 cities in the U.S.

Usage

home.prices

Format

A data frame with 21 observations on the following 3 variables.

City
name of the city
y1985
average home price in 1985
y2000
average home price in 2000

Source

2001 New York Times Almanac
