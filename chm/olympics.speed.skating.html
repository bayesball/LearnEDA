olympics.speed.skating(LearnEDA)R Documentation

Olympics speed skating times.

Description

Winning time in Olympics mens speed skating race classified by length of race and year.

Usage

olympics.speed.skating

Format

A data frame with 9 observations on the following 6 variables.

YEAR
year of the Olympics
X500m
winning time in seconds for 500m
X1000m
winning time in seconds for 1000m
X1500m
winning time in seconds for 1500m
X5000m
winning time in seconds fro 5000m
X10000m
winning time in seconds for 10000m

Source

World Almanac and Book of Facts 2008
