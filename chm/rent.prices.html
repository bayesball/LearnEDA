rent.prices(LearnEDA)R Documentation

Rent prices in different cities.

Description

Rental prices (different types from different cities).

Usage

rent.prices

Format

A data frame with 9 observations on the following 6 variables.

City
city
Studio
rent of a studio apartment
One.Bedroom
rent of a one bedroom apartment
Two.Bedroom
rent of a two bedroom apartment
Three.Bedroom
rent of a three bedroom apartment
Four.Bedroom
rent of a four bedroom apartment

Source

2001 New York Times Almanac
