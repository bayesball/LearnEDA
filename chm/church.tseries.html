church.tseries(LearnEDA)R Documentation

Time series of worship attendance

Description

Time series of weekly worship attendance from a church in Ohio, U.S.

Usage

church.tseries

Format

A data frame with 209 observations on the following 3 variables.

year
the year
month
the month
worship
count of worship attendance
