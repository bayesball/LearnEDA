pop.change(LearnEDA)R Documentation

Population change for all states in the U.S.

Description

Population change for all states in the United States between the years 1900 and 1999.

Usage

pop.change

Format

A data frame with 51 observations on the following 2 variables.

State
state abbreviation
Pct.change
percentage change in population

Source

World Almanac and Book of Facts 2001
