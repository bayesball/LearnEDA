mortality.rates(LearnEDA)R Documentation

Infant mortality rates of countries

Description

Infant mortality rates in 1997 for 68 countries (Afghanistan through Guyana).

Usage

mortality.rates

Format

A data frame with 68 observations on the following 1 variable.

rate
infant mortality rate

Source

2001 New York Times Almanac
