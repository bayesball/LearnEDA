heaviest.fish(LearnEDA)R Documentation

Fish world record catches

Description

Weights in pounds of world record catches of saltwater fish.

Usage

heaviest.fish

Format

A data frame with 25 observations on the following 2 variables.

Species
name of country
Weight
region of the world

Source

World Almanac and Book of Facts 2008
