island.areas(LearnEDA)R Documentation

Areas of islands from different continents.

Description

Total number of home runs hit all teams in the major league baseball league for the years 1900,1910,..., 2000.

Usage

islands.areas

Format

A data frame with 59 observations on the following 3 variables.

Ocean
ocean where the island lies
Name
name of the island
Area
area of the island

Source

World Almanac and Book of Facts
