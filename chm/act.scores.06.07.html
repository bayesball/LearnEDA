act.scores.06.07(LearnEDA)R Documentation

ACT scores of states in the US.

Description

Average ACT composite scores for all states for the 2006-07 year.

Usage

act.scores.06.07

Format

A data frame with 51 observations on the following 5 variables.

STATE
name of state
Abbrev
state abbreviation
ACT
average ACT composite score
Per.Grads
percentage of students who took the ACT exam
Region
region of the United States

Source

World Almanac and Book of Facts 2008
